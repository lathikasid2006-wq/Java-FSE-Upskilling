About the Portal

Community Bulletin

The city council has launched this portal to help residents stay connected with local events. Whether you are interested in cultural festivals, sports meets, health camps, or skill workshops, this is your one-stop platform. We aim to make community participation simple and accessible for everyone. New events are added every week so keep checking back for updates. Volunteers are also welcome to help organize and manage upcoming events across different neighborhoods.
