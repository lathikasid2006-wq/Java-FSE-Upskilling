How to Use This Portal

Registration

Fill in all required fields in the registration form and click Register. You will see a confirmation message.

Finding Events Near You

Click the "Find Nearby Events" button and allow location access when prompted by your browser.

Preferences

Your selected event type is saved automatically. You can clear it anytime using the "Clear Preferences" button.
